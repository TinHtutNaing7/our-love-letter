My Dearest Love,

There are moments in life so quietly perfect that no photograph can hold them — moments I find only in your eyes when you laugh. I have kept every single one of them, folded gently inside my heart.

When I think of us, I think of mornings — the kind where time stretches slowly and the whole world feels like it belongs only to us. I think of your voice saying my name and how nothing before that sound ever felt like home.

You have taught me that love is not a grand gesture performed once — it is every ordinary Tuesday, every shared silence, every cup of tea made just the way I like it. It is the quiet miracle of being truly known by another soul.

On this anniversary, I want you to know: I would choose you in every lifetime, in every world, without a single hesitation. You are the answer to every question I never knew I was asking.
